Fallout: New Vegas Cheat Codes

Cheat codes in Fallout: New Vegas only work on PC and Mac — console commands aren't supported on PS3/Xbox 360. Press ~ (tilde/backtick, the key below Esc) to open the console. Some commands require you to click a character first to select a target.

God Mode & Invincibility

God Mode (Invincibility Including Infinite Ammo and Carry Weight)
tgm
Demigod Mode (Can't die, but ammo runs out and you still reload)
tdm

Movement & Camera

Walk Through Walls Mode (Noclip)
tcl
Free Flying Camera
tfc

Items & Caps

Add 1,000 Caps
player.additem 000000F 1000
Add a Specific Item to Inventory (enter Item ID and quantity)
player.additem [ItemID] [Quantity]

Character Progression

Set Level to a Specific Value (e.g. 30)
player.setlevel 30
Increase a S.P.E.C.I.A.L. Stat (e.g. Strength)
player.modav strength 10
Increase Karma Points
player.modav karma 1000

NPC & Companions

Spawn an NPC/Companion Next to You (enter NPC ID)
player.placeatme [NPC_ID]

Reputation & Factions

Set Faction Reputation
setreputation [FactionID] [Value]
Increase Faction Reputation
addreputation [FactionID] [Value]
Become an Ally of a Faction
addtofaction [FactionID] 1

Quests

Complete a Specific Quest (enter Quest ID)
completequest [QuestID]
Advance a Quest to a Specific Stage
setstage [QuestID] [Stage]
Reset a Quest (For Stuck Quests)
resetquest [QuestID]

World & Misc

Unlock All Fast Travel Points
tmm 1
Teleport to a Specific Location (enter Cell ID)
coc [CellID]

Item/NPC/Faction/Quest IDs are found in in-game wiki resources — each has its own unique code, you'll need to add the correct ID yourself.
