Minecraft Cheat Codes

Minecraft uses an in-game command system instead of "cheat codes." All commands below are identical on PC, PS4, and PS5 — the only difference is how you enter the command.

On Java Edition, turn on "Allow Cheats: ON" when creating your world, or enable it in an existing world via Esc > Open to LAN. On Bedrock Edition, go to Settings > Game > turn on "Activate Cheats." Press T or / to open chat, paste the command, press Enter.

Turn on "Activate Cheats" from the Settings > Game tab. Press up on the D-pad to open chat, type (or paste) the command with the on-screen keyboard, press submit.
