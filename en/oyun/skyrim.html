Skyrim Cheat Codes

Cheat codes in Skyrim only work on PC and Mac — console commands aren't supported on PS4/PS5/Xbox. Press ~ (tilde/backtick, the key below Esc) to open the console. Some commands (like kill, resurrect) require you to click a character first to select a target.
